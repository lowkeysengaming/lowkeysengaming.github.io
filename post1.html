[ EVENT ] Cap Race, Phase 4 Feb 01 ~ 29
Administrator

Promotions details:

1. Rookie’s Contest
By the end of promotion, GM will rank the TOP 10 new accounts that registered after the start of promotion.
First runner up will be rewarded with  31,888 epoints ,
2nd place with  23,888 epoints ,
3rd place with  15,888 epoints ,
4th to 10th place with  7,888 epoints .
All accounts registered before the start of promotion are not qualified for this promotion.
We recommend that players quit the game once 15 minutes before the end of the event, so that your character reborn and level will be saved and calculated in the contest.


2. Online Time Contest
By the end of promotion, GM will compute the accumulated online time of the characters
from the start of promotion to the end and rank the top 10 accounts.
First runner up will be rewarded with  31,888 epoints ,
2nd place with  23,888 epoints ,
3rd place with  15,888 epoints ,
4th to 10th place with  7,888 epoints .
All accounts whether previously or newly registered are qualified for this promotion.
We recommend that players quit the game once 15 minutes before the end of the event, so that account online time will be saved and calculated in the contest.


3. EP Donation Contest
By the end of promotion, GM will compute epoint donation from the start of promotion
to the end of each account and rank the top 10 accounts.
First runner up will be rewarded with  31,888 epoints ,
2nd place with  23,888 epoints ,
3rd place with  15,888 epoints ,
4th to 10th place with  7,888 epoints .
All accounts whether previously or newly registered are qualified for this promotion.
